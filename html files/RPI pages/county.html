Economic

RPI contributes more than 1 billion dollars annually to the Capital District. This also goes along with the jobs that are created by RPI. The students are also actively working for the companies in the area. To see the full economic impact of RPI, go to the Economic section of RPI in Troy.

Community

RPI provides community service to Troy and the area around it. Whether it is cleaning the streets, educating the youth, or help and skills for people, the institute is always helping the community.

Helping Others

RPI and the community have come together many times before in raising money through RPI Relief. It started in 2005 after Hurricane Katrina. Together, $35,000 was raised for American Red Cross and other organizations. Since then, they have also raised money for Hurricane Irene, the Japanese Earthquake and Tsunami, Hurricane Sandy, and many others.
