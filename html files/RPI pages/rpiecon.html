Contribution

An article from 2015 that can be found on RPI’s website states that RPI contributes more than 1 billion dollars annually to the Capital Region. This includes job creation, student spending, visitor spending, and research. Thousands of jobs, direct and indirect, are supported by RPI.

Local Impact

The city of Troy and the surrounding Capital District see the impact of students living close by. Whether it is going out to eat, going to the farmer’s market, or looking for something fun to do, many college kids are able to help support these businesses. The students are also able to help these businesses that are close by through internships and jobs.

Global Impact

RPI also impacts the global economy. With RPI being a research-heavy school, many innovations and advancements have been made. These advancements impact the industries and can lead to economic impacts. Also, many students go off to work for big companies such as Amazon, Google, or Microsoft. These big companies are a big part of today’s world and are always looking for ways to be better.
